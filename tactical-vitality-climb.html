About this game

What is the Tactical Vitality Climb?

It is a free Teach-Then-Play learning game from Global Sovereign University. Each round teaches one real beat of first aid and emergency calm in a short card, then asks you to use it. Correct answers carry you up a Bronze, Silver, Gold, and Platinum climb. There is no way to fail, and you can play forever.

Does this replace a real first-aid course?

No. The game builds awareness, vocabulary, and the calm to act, and it makes you a far more prepared learner. It is not a substitute for certified hands-on training or professional medical care. In a real emergency, call your local emergency number (911 in the U.S.) first and follow the instructions of trained responders.

Is it suitable for kids and families?

Yes. Like the book it is built on, the game is written plainly enough for a young reader and complete enough for the adults beside them. It teaches calm-under-pressure first, then practical skills, with no graphic content — ideal for families and homeschoolers learning together.

Who is GENO?

GENO is GSU's free 24/7 AI mentor. He has read the entire Tactical Vitality book, not just a preview, so you can ask him anything — the 3-2-1 rule, how to run CMS checks, the Chain of Survival, or any question you missed in the climb.
