Every Game GSU Has Built · Under One Roof

Eight aisles. Dozens of games. Every one of them free forever, built by teachers who believe children learn faster when the lesson feels like a game they cannot lose.

Rate the Arcade
Why We Play

The founders of this republic learned by drilling, playing, arguing, and making things. Books were second. The workshop, the field, the debate, and the game came first. Play is not the opposite of learning. Play is the shape learning takes when the learner is free.

Every game in this arcade was built to that principle. No advertising. No paywall. No stopwatch that punishes you for thinking. Just the game, the lesson, and the door back home when you are ready.

The Curated Arcade · a note from the builder

The geneconstant36-blip/gsu-assets repo contains 217 game and interactive pages at last enumeration. This arcade curates approximately 40 flagship titles across the eight aisles — the ones every citizen should walk through first. The rest live in the repo and remain reachable directly.

The Foundation's philosophy on games: every game is free forever. No advertising, no paywall, no data harvesting. Play for the knowledge gained. If a game has stayed with you for a week, you have already received what the game was for.
