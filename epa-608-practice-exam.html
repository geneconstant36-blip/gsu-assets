Frequently Asked Questions

Is this the real EPA 608 exam?

No. It is a free practice exam with original questions written to the EPA Section 608 published topic areas. Actual certification is issued only by EPA-approved certifying organizations.

What score passes the real exam?

The widely used standard is 70% per section — the same bar this practice exam applies to its readiness verdict.

What do the four sections cover?

Core: regulations, ozone science, safety, recovery basics — required of everyone. Type I: small appliances (5 lb or less). Type II: high-pressure appliances like residential split systems. Type III: low-pressure appliances like centrifugal chillers. Pass Core plus all three types and you're eligible for Universal.

Is the readiness certificate an EPA credential?

No — it documents that you scored 70% or better in every section of this practice exam. A study milestone, honestly labeled.
