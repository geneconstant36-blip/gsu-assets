Plain-Folk Instructions

Build Your First Stick in Fifteen Minutes

  1. DOWNLOAD. On any connected computer, click the gold button above and download the free ZIP file.
  2. UNZIP. Right-click the file → “Extract All” (Windows) or double-click it (Mac). A folder appears.
  3. COPY. Drag the whole folder onto any USB flash drive. A laptop folder, a tablet's files, a school network drive, or a burned DVD all work the same.
  4. OPEN. On any computer — connected or not — open the folder and double-click index.html. The campus opens in the browser.
  5. TEACH. Pick a game. Bronze for beginners, Platinum for the proud. The explanations do the teaching.

The golden rule: if it can open a webpage, it can host the university.
