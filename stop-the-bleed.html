Stop the Bleed.

Uncontrolled bleeding is the number one preventable cause of death after injury — a person can bleed to death in minutes. The hands nearest them are the ones most likely to save them. Yours. Learn the three moves that buy time, or stand in when no help is coming.

A — AlertCall your local emergency number (911 · 112 · 999 · 000), or send someone to call.
B — BleedingFind the source. Open or remove clothing — blood hides under fabric.
C — CompressPressure, packing, or a tourniquet — stop the flow.
