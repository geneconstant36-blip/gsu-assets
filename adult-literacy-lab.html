Adult Literacy Lab

This lab is not for children. It is for every adult who finished school without the reading skills they were promised — and who is ready to learn the code they were never taught.

You weren't bad at reading.
You were taught wrong.
For decades, American schools taught reading by asking children to guess words from pictures and context — a method called whole language. The science of reading shows this approach fails the majority of learners, particularly those who needed systematic phonics. If you struggled in school, the failure was not yours. It was the method's. This lab gives you the code you were never given — the same code the research says every reader needs — delivered with the dignity you deserve as an adult.
