Questions, answered.

How do I play?

Pick a book. Read the short lesson, then spell: you type each word from its clue and pronunciation — you build it yourself, you don't pick from a list. Every correct word lifts you from Bronze toward Platinum. Play is infinite; bank your score whenever you like.

Is it really free?

Yes — every book, every game, GENO, and the certificate. GSU is a nonprofit. No cost, no ads, no login, ever.

What is the "When Spelling Won't Stick" deck?

It's the no-fail deck, built for dyslexic, dysgraphic, and struggling spellers. No clock, no losing — if a word slips, it reveals itself letter by letter and you try again, gently, until it sticks.

What if a word stumps me?

Tap "Show a letter" for a nudge, or ask GENO in the corner — he's read every book and will walk you through the pattern in your language.
