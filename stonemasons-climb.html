About this game

What is the Stonemasons Climb?

It is a free Teach-Then-Play learning game from Global Sovereign University. Each round teaches one real beat of the masonry craft in a short card, then asks you to use it. Correct answers carry you up a Bronze, Silver, Gold, and Platinum climb. There is no way to fail, and you can play forever.

Do I need any masonry experience?

None at all. The game starts with the very basics of a clean work site and builds toward arches, dry-stack walls, veneer, and restoration. Every question is preceded by the short lesson that answers it, so you are always taught before you are tested.

Is it really free?

Yes. The game, the full book it is based on, and GENO the mentor are all free from Global Sovereign University. GSU's mission is a hand up, not a handout — the tools of competence belong to anyone willing to pick them up.

Who is GENO?

GENO is GSU's free 24/7 AI mentor. He has read the entire Stonemasons book, not just a preview, so you can ask him anything — about a question you missed, a tool, a mortar type, or how a real wall is built — and he will explain it in plain language.
