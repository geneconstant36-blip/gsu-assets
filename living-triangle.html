The trade secret hiding inside every right triangle

The Pythagorean theorem is a daily tool. a² + b² = c² — that's every rafter, every stair stringer, every diagonal brace. If you know any two sides, you know the third. If your triangle satisfies 3-4-5 (or 6-8-10, or 9-12-15), your corner is square.

Roof pitch is rise over run. A "6 in 12" pitch means: for every 12 inches of horizontal run, the roof rises 6 inches. That ratio determines rafter length, cut angles, and how snow loads.
