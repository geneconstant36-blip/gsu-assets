Key Concepts — Behavior Series

The N400

A neural spike 400ms after incongruent speech — the brain's integration alarm. Affective incongruence is measurable electricity.

Bateson's Double Bind

Two contradictory messages with no escape and no permission to name the contradiction. Not just confusing — reality-fracturing.

The Mehrabian Myth

Words are not 7% of communication. That number was derived from a narrow 1967 study about emotional attitude — not general conversation.

The Othello Error

Interpreting an innocent person's fear as proof of guilt. The observer's own suspicion creates the arousal they then treat as evidence.

Physiological Flooding

When the autonomic nervous system ramps high enough that semantic processing gets squeezed. You cannot argue someone out of flooding.

The Three C's

Context · Clusters · Congruence. How to read nonverbal behavior without folklore, without courtrooms, and without the Othello Error.
