Your first steps

Four small steps. Go slow. There is no test, and no one is watching.
Press the button and we will read every step out loud.
1

Talk to GENO

Press the button and just say out loud: “I want to learn to read.” GENO listens and walks with you. You don’t have to read anything to begin.

🎤  Talk to GENO
2

Learn the sounds

Letters are just sounds you can see. We start with the sounds — slowly, one at a time.

▶  Start with sounds
3

Build small words

Put the sounds together into small words. Cat. Sun. Run. You will surprise yourself.

▶  Build words
4

Read something real

A sign. A note. A short story. The day you read your first sentence on your own — come back and tell GENO. We will celebrate with you.

▶  Read with us

Go at your own speed. Five minutes a day is enough to change a life. (Soon, you’ll even be able to learn by phone — we’re building that for you now.)
