The Core Idea

Every human interaction runs on two channels at once. The explicit channel carries words. The implicit channel carries tone, posture, timing, and micro-expression. When they align — congruence. When they conflict — the brain flags it as a threat. Your nervous system detects this in milliseconds, before you know what you noticed.

How to Play

You will read 10 real-world communication scenarios. For each one, identify what is actually happening neurologically, psychologically, or practically. The N400, the Othello Error, Bateson's Double Bind, Rogers' Congruence, Mehrabian's Myth, and more — all in action.
