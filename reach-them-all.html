The Mission

Reach Them All.

Most of the world is not at a screen. Reach Them All carries a free tutor and a full library to the people the internet forgot — by phone, and by box. Free. Worldwide. In 32 languages.

We say free education for everyone. Then we build it on a website — and quietly leave most of humanity standing on the wrong side of a screen they cannot reach. Billions live with no reliable internet. Many have no smartphone. Many cannot read the screen even if you handed them one.

So GSU built two ways to reach them anyway: the GENO Hotline, a free tutor you call by voice in your own language — and GENO in a Box, an offline library and tutor for the places a signal never reaches. Not charity. A tool placed in a capable hand. The promise was everyone — and we intend to keep it.
