How to Use This Atlas

1. If you don't know where to start: Work the four Foundation skills first. Every subject sits on top of them. A Learner who is fluent, numerate, has working vocabulary, and shows up daily will pass more certifications than one who is brilliant on one and shaky on the rest.

2. If you know which certification you want: Click that certification's tile. The map will show you the specific skills that certification tests beyond the Foundation, and where each skill is taught. Work top-down.

3. If you already took the diagnostic: The Where Do I Stand? tool flagged the subjects you were not ready for. Come here, click those certifications, and start with whichever prerequisite skill you missed on. That is your road out.

4. If you get stuck at any step: Ask GENO on this page. He has the entire text of many GSU books memorized and speaks 83 languages. Tell him which skill you are working and he will walk you through it with two worked examples.
