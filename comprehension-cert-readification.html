How the certification works

  • 25 questions drawn at random from a 50-question bank — 5 from each of the 5 Reading Helix pillars
  • 80% (20 of 25) to pass — Bronze 80-84%, Silver 85-89%, Gold 90-94%, Platinum 95-100%
  • One retest if you score 70-79% on the first attempt
  • Anonymous — no account creation, no email required, no data extracted
  • Verifiable — every certificate has a unique serial, lookupable by anyone at the verification page
  • Permanent record — once issued, a certificate is immutable in our database
