How the Road Works

  • At each gate, you read a passage. Then you answer one comprehension question about it.
  • On the Best road, the passage is dense and the question probes inference, tone, or synthesis. Right answer → continue on Best. Wrong → the road below rises. You continue on Better.
  • On the Better road, passages are direct. Questions test clear meaning. Wrong → you continue on Good.
  • On the Good road, you cannot be demoted. You will finish. That is the point of Good.
  • Three endings await you. Master Comprehension, at the end of the Best road. The Sharp Reader, at the end of the Better road. The Confident Reader, at the end of the Good road. All three are counted. All three earn certificates.
