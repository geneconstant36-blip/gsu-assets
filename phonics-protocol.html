Questions about phonics

The reading code, in plain words.

What is phonics?

Phonics is the link between letters and the sounds they make. Once you know the code, you can sound out and read words you have never seen before.

Who is Phonics Protocol for?

Anyone learning to read — children or adults, anywhere in the world. It is completely free.

How does the game teach reading?

You learn by doing. You hear the sounds, build words from their parts, and decode real words, with instant feedback and the rule explained on every try.

Does the game ever end?

No. Phonics Protocol is endless practice — the words keep coming and your score keeps climbing through Bronze, Silver, Gold, and Platinum.

Is it really free?

Yes. It is free with no signup and no login, from Global Sovereign University, a 501(c)(3) nonprofit.

Do I need to read already?

No. Tap the speaker button to hear words read aloud, so you can play and learn before you can read on your own.
