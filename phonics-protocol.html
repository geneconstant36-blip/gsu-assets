How It Works

The Phonics Protocol uses a combinatorial question engine — a small set of phonics rules generates an unlimited number of unique questions. You'll never see the same question twice.

Bronze — Letter-sound basics. Single consonants, short vowels, CVC words. The foundation everything else builds on.

Silver — Consonant blends, digraphs (sh, ch, th), and long vowel patterns. You start decoding real words.

Gold — R-controlled vowels, diphthongs, silent letters, and multi-syllable decoding. Real-world reading territory.

Platinum — Greek and Latin roots, morpheme analysis, and advanced decoding. You don't just read — you reverse-engineer words.

Each tier: 10 questions. Score 80%+ to unlock the next tier. Your progress is tracked in your browser.
