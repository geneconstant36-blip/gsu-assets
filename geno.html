About GENO

Is GENO really free?

Yes. GSU is a 501(c)(3) educational foundation. GENO is free to everyone, everywhere — no ads, no login, no paywall. Free, forever.

Does GENO replace teachers?

No. GENO does not replace the human relationships that make a learner. He makes sure no one has to wait for one. He is the tutor available at midnight, in your language, when no human teacher is in the room.

What can GENO help me with?

Every subject at GSU — reading, writing, math, money, civics, trades, science, history, and digital skills. He can read aloud, explain, quiz you, and guide you to the right free resource.

What languages does GENO speak?

Thirty-two. He comprehends English, Spanish, and Chinese with particular depth, and answers in the language you speak.
