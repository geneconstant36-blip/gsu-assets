Is this really free, with no account?

Yes — free forever, anonymous, no login. The Foundation for Global Instruction is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit. The optional name on the certificate is the only thing you type, and it is your choice.

How does the verification work?

When you pass, a unique serial number is generated and recorded. Anyone can paste that serial into the box above to confirm the subject, the tier (Silver or Gold), and the date — proving the certificate is real without revealing any personal information.

Can I retake it?

As many times as you like, free. A new random set of 30 questions is drawn each attempt, so it rewards real comprehension, not memorizing one form.
