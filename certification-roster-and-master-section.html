The Master of Comprehension

Every certificate on this page is a step toward one award. Earn every active GSU Comprehension Certification, then face the Capstone Crossing — thirty questions drawn at random across all of your subjects, eighty percent to pass — and join the smallest honor roll on the campus.

1 · Climb Them AllEarn the Certificate of Comprehension in every active subject. Your record appends as new subjects launch — it never resets.
2 · The Capstone CrossingOne final session: thirty questions spanning every subject you completed, at least two from each. This proves retention, not momentum.
3 · Class of Your YearMasters are recorded by cohort — Class of 2026 will be the smallest there will ever be — and may opt in to the public honor roll.

"Master of Comprehension — [Name] — Class of [Year] — [N] Subjects — Verified."

Every Master receives a standing invitation to become a Civilization Builders mentor — because the natural next step after mastering comprehension is helping someone else find theirs.
