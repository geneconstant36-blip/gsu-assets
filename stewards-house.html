Questions, answered

How does The Steward’s House work?Eight household emergencies — a tripping breaker, a gas smell, a 2 a.m. toilet — each a three-decision story. You assess, diagnose, and act; every choice teaches the Sovereign Steward method: diagnose before paying, safety before speed, and the boundary where licensed pros take over. Dangerous choices reset your Safe Streak and explain exactly why. The scenarios reshuffle forever.

Is this a repair manual?It’s a judgment trainer — the companion game to Foundations of Repair, which carries the method in full. Education only: always de-energize, depressurize, and verify; gas lines, main panels, and structural work belong to licensed professionals, in this game and in your house.

Is it really free?Yes — free, no login, no data harvested. Scores save anonymously so your streak survives between visits. GSU operates under The Foundation for Global Instruction, a 501(c)(3) nonprofit.

What should I do next?Read the anchor text, walk the seven-book Sovereign Trades shelf on the Tradification hub, and tonight: go locate your home’s main water shut-off. That one is homework.
