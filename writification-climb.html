About this game

What is The Story Climb?

It is a free Teach-Then-Play creative-writing game from Global Sovereign University. Each round teaches one beat of storytelling craft in a short card, then asks you to use it on real writing. Correct calls carry you up a Bronze, Silver, Gold, and Platinum climb. There is no way to fail, you can play forever, and spaced repetition brings the beats you miss back around more often so they stick.

Will this actually make me a better writer?

It builds the instincts behind good storytelling — character and want, tension and stakes, vivid detail, strong verbs, and your own voice — and the habit of writing without fear. It is a coach for the moves, not a replacement for the one thing every author must do: write your own stories. Use it alongside putting real words on your own page.

Is it good for kids and homeschoolers?

Yes. It is plain enough for a young writer and deep enough for the adult beside them, and every question asks you to judge real writing rather than recall trivia. It is ideal for families, homeschoolers, and anyone rebuilding confidence on the page.

Who is GENO?

GENO is GSU's free 24/7 AI writing mentor — a robot you can actually talk to, in 32 languages. Ask him to explain show-don't-tell, suggest a stronger verb, help you find your character's want, or talk through any beat you missed.
