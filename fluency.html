Fluency

The reading that sounds like talking, while the meaning keeps moving.
Coming soon · Free, always

Decoding builds the toolkit. Fluency is what happens after — when the brain stops working the word and starts living inside the sentence.

A fluent reader does not just read each word accurately. They read in phrases. They hear the punctuation in their own voice. They sense when a sentence is rising toward a question and when it is settling into a quiet conclusion. Comprehension stops being a task they do after reading and starts happening while they read.
