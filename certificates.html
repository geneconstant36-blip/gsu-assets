Achievement & Recognition

Free to learn. Yours to prove.

At Global Sovereign University, the education is free — and so is the proof. When you are ready, you can sit a short exam and earn a Certificate of Comprehension: a personalized, verifiable certificate with your name on it, delivered straight to your inbox.

Explore the Program

Why this matters

Most people who teach themselves something have nothing to show for it but their own quiet knowledge. They studied hard, late at night, with no classroom and no teacher — and then the world asked them to prove it, and they had no paper in their hand.

We think that is a small injustice worth fixing. So alongside the free learning, GSU offers a free way to be recognized for it. Pass the exam, and you hold the proof. It is not an accredited degree, and we will never pretend it is. It is something simpler and honest: evidence that you sat down, did the work, and demonstrated that you understand.

How you earn your certificate

1

Learn, for free

Study the subject using GSU’s free books, courses, games, and GENO, your AI tutor. Take as long as you need; nothing is timed until you choose to begin.

2

Take the exam

When you feel ready, sit the exam: 30 questions in 30 minutes. It is open to anyone — no enrollment, no fee, no gatekeeping.

3

Pass at 80%

Answer 80 percent correctly and you have earned your Certificate of Comprehension in that subject.

4

Receive it by email

Your certificate arrives as a personalized PDF — your name, the subject, the date — free to download, print, and frame.

5

Prove it is real

Every certificate carries a unique serial number, and a public verification page lets anyone confirm it is genuine. Your achievement is yours, and it is checkable.

A keepsake to hold, too: your certificate is digital first — yours to print and frame today — and a mailed keepsake certificate is part of our plan as the program grows.

What you can be certified in

The first Certificates of Comprehension launch on July 4, 2026. From there the program grows, subject by subject, until there is a certificate for every faculty of the university:

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the GSU Certificate of Comprehension?
It is a certificate you earn by passing a short exam that shows you understand a subject. It carries your name and a unique serial number, and anyone can confirm it is genuine through a public verification page.
Is the certificate free?
Yes. As with everything at GSU, both the learning and the certificate are free. It is a recognition of demonstrated understanding, not an accredited degree.
What is the exam like?
Thirty questions in thirty minutes, with a passing score of 80 percent. You can study the subject for free beforehand and take the exam when you feel ready.
How do I receive my certificate?
As a personalized PDF delivered to your email — free to download, print, and frame, with your name and the date you earned it.
Can someone confirm my certificate is real?
Yes. Each certificate carries a unique serial number, and a public verification page lets anyone — an employer, a family member, or you — confirm that it is genuine.
When can I earn one?
The first Certificates of Comprehension launch on July 4, 2026, with more subjects added over time until there is a certificate for every faculty.
Will there be a physical, mailed certificate?
The certificate is digital first — yours to print and frame the moment you earn it. A mailed keepsake certificate is part of our plan as the program grows.
