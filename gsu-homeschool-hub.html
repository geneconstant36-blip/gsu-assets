The classroom punished the way your child's brain works.

Boys are diagnosed with ADHD at 2–10 times the rate of girls in institutional classrooms — and placed in special education at disproportionate rates. The energy, the curiosity, the inability to sit still for six hours — these are not defects. They are mismatches.

GSU's game-based model is self-paced, challenge-driven, and entirely free of the time pressure that compounds ADHD anxiety. GENO waits. There is no bell. There is no wrong way to pace a BookGame.

Dyslexic learners require self-paced, multi-sensory instruction without the artificial time pressure of standardized classrooms. The Phonics Protocol — GSU's combinatorial reading engine — was built specifically for this. Infinite practice. No shame. No clock.

GENO reads aloud in 32 languages. Every coaching note can be spoken. The Phonics Protocol generates unlimited reading practice from finite phonetic rules — no two sessions are identical.
