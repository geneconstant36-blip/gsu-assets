Questions

What does this game actually teach?

The one skill every trade shares: systematic diagnosis. You read a real symptom, choose the next best move — safety first, cheapest and most-likely cause first — and GENO explains the reasoning after every step. You learn by doing the thinking, not by memorizing trivia.

How does the leaderboard work?

It's fully anonymous. You pick a handle, and your best score competes head-to-head with learners worldwide on a Today board and an All-Time board. No demographics, no income, no postal code — only merit. A child with a borrowed phone can out-rank anyone on Earth.

What are combos, streaks, and the daily repair?

String correct calls together to raise your combo multiplier — bigger combos mean bigger points. The Daily Repair is a fresh challenge each day; solve it to keep your day streak alive. And any repair you get wrong will quietly come back until you conquer it.

Will this make me a licensed tradesperson?

No — and it isn't meant to. It builds safe, general judgment and confidence. For gas, major electrical, or structural work, always call a licensed professional. The game reminds you exactly where that line is.
