The Information Farm

Watch information flow from source to screen — then turn on the filters, the algorithm, and the wall, and see what reaches the reader. This is how your bubble forms: not with violence, but with convenience.

The farm doesn't need guards — it has algorithms

Every piece of content travels through filters you didn't choose. Platform algorithms, government censors, corporate sponsors, and your own click history all shape what you see. The dangerous part: filtered information feels complete. You don't see what's missing.

The filter bubble is self-reinforcing. You click what matches your views → the algorithm shows you more of the same → your views harden → you click more of the same. This loop requires no conspiracy — just optimization for engagement.
