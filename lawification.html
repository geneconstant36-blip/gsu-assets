The Law Helix

Know the law,

Most adults cannot read the documents they are asked to sign — and a person who cannot read the rules cannot defend their rights. The Law Helix teaches practical legal literacy on five pillars, plain enough to use the next time a contract lands on your table.

The Foundation

Five pillars of legal literacy.

You do not need a law degree to stop being intimidated by the fine print of your own life. These five pillars are the working knowledge of a citizen who cannot be easily taken advantage of.

I

The Rule of Law

The founding documents and the hierarchy of law — how law is made, ranked, and applied, from the Constitution down.

II

Reading the Fine Print

The single most practical legal skill: understanding the contracts, leases, and forms you are asked to sign.

III

Your Rights

What your rights actually protect — with police, at work, as a consumer — and how to assert them calmly and correctly.

IV

Standing Up for Yourself

Using the system without a fortune in fees — small claims, legal aid, and self-representation.

V

Legal Judgment

Thinking through a legal problem — spotting the issue, finding the rule, and knowing the moment to call a professional.
