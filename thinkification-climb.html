Questions, answered

How does the Climb work?Questions climb from Bronze to Gold and never run out — the deck reshuffles forever, your streak builds your score, and every answer teaches: each question ends with the why, not just the what.

Is it really free?Yes — free, no login, no data harvested. Scores save anonymously so your climb survives between visits. GSU operates under The Foundation for Global Instruction, a 501(c)(3) nonprofit.

What should I do next?Pick one mental model from the game and apply it to a real decision this week — then visit Critical Thinking for the defensive half of the toolkit.
