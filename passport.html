Questions

What is the Climber's Passport?

It's your personal record of progress across Global Sovereign University's free games and lessons. Every climb you begin and every badge you earn lives here, ready on any device you sign in from. It's yours to keep — a tool, not a token someone can take away.

How do I earn a badge?

Each lesson awards four badges. Bronze is for finishing the lesson itself; Silver, Gold, and Platinum are earned by doing real things in the world — the homework reps that turn knowledge into a habit. Mark each one here as you earn it, and your Passport remembers it for good.

Do I need an account or a password?

No. The Passport signs you in anonymously and saves your progress to a private space automatically. There's nothing to buy, no email to give, and no one else can see or change your record.

Where do the best scores come from?

Straight from your own play. Each climb saves your best score to the cloud under your private ID, and the Passport reads it back. Beat your best in any game and it updates here automatically.

Can I print it?

Yes — tap "Print my Passport" for a clean, dated certificate of everything you've earned. Show it to a parent, a co-op, or an employer. Finishing every climb completes the campus.
