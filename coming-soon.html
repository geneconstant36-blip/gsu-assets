New Pillar

Health & Vitality

The body is the first thing you have to be able to rely on. Built around The Lifeline Track — life-saving skill for when help is far away, or not coming at all.

  • LifelineAnatomy for the rescuer, bleeding control, choking & the Heimlich, CPR and the defibrillator, splinting a break, and care over the long hours.
  • Preventative HealthClean water, sanitation, hygiene, and nutrition — the basics that prevent most of what harms a body anywhere on Earth.
  • Occupational FitnessConditioning and ergonomics for a lifetime of physical work, without breaking down.
  • Mental ResilienceStaying clear and deciding well under pressure — and recovering afterward.
Tradification · New Tier

Off-Grid & Self-Reliant Infrastructure

An expansion of GSU's trades pillar — beyond home repair into building and running your own environment, anywhere in the world.

  • AgriculturePermaculture, hydroponics, and soil regeneration — how to grow and scale your own food supply. A person who can feed themselves is free.
  • Off-Grid PowerSolar, battery banks, and micro-hydro — electricity that answers to no one.
  • Water InfrastructureRainwater harvesting, well maintenance, and greywater recycling.
  • Structural FabricationWelding, masonry, and building with the earth — adobe, rammed earth, and climate-fit construction.
About the medical training: The Lifeline Track teaches what an ordinary person can safely do to preserve life and limb until professional care is reached. Every badge is a Comprehension Certificate — not a medical certification or license. It is a supplement to professional care, never a replacement for it. Reach trained help whenever you can.

You won't learn it alone. GENO, the GSU AI tutor, is available 24/7 — a tutor you can actually talk to — coaching every lesson and game in 32 languages.
