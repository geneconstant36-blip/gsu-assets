Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Sovereign Transcript?

A single page that gathers every serial-numbered GSU Certificate of Comprehension you have earned into one unified, printable, shareable document. It is free, requires no account, and every line is verified live against GSU's public certificate registry the moment the page loads.

How does verification work?

Your link contains only serial numbers — no claims. When anyone opens it, their own browser queries the public registry directly and confirms each serial: subject, score, tier, and issue date. A line only shows VERIFIED if the registry itself confirms it, so the transcript cannot be forged, edited, or inflated.

Is my transcript private?

Completely under your control. Certificates are earned anonymously under a name you choose, and your transcript exists only as a link you build and decide whether to share. Assembling it uploads nothing; the page reads the same public registry employers already use to verify individual certificates.
