The Law Behind All Machines

Work = Force × Distance. Machines don't reduce the work required — they redistribute it. You can apply less force over a greater distance, and accomplish the same work. That ratio is called Mechanical Advantage (MA).

MA = Output Force ÷ Input Force. A MA of 5 means you apply 5× less force than the load — but you must push/pull 5× further. Nothing is free. The machine just makes the job possible.
