The Information Farm

Every piece of information you receive has passed through a filter before it reached you. Some filters are obvious. Most are invisible. This animation shows the journey of a "truth particle" from source to your screen — and what happens to it along the way.

How the Farm Works

In George Orwell's Animal Farm, the pigs controlled what the other animals believed by controlling what they heard. The Digital Farm is the modern version: algorithms, platform rules, advertiser pressure, engagement optimization, and echo chambers all act as filters between raw reality and your perception of it.

A sovereign thinker understands these filters. They don't just consume information — they trace it back to the source, ask who benefits from them believing it, and seek out the view they weren't supposed to see.
