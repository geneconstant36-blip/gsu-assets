Before You Play — Understand This

Every roof, every ramp, every staircase, every brace in a building is a right triangle. The three sides have names that carpenters, framers, and electricians use every single day:

Rise = how far up. Run = how far across. Hypotenuse = the diagonal — always the longest side, always opposite the right angle.

The Pythagorean theorem says: rise² + run² = hypotenuse². Drag the triangle and watch that equation prove itself — live — every time you move a point.
