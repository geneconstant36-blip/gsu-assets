🐟 What Is a Red Herring?

• Red herring: Any argument or topic that distracts from the main issue.

• Origin: The term comes from using smelly fish to distract hunting dogs from a scent trail.

• Purpose: To avoid answering difficult questions or admitting fault.

• Counter: "That's interesting, but let's get back to the original question..."
