Questions, answered

Does Civicification take a political side?No. The curriculum teaches the system, not a side — built to work for the citizen who votes red, blue, neither, or has never voted at all. The books examine the Constitution, civic virtue, rights, and citizenship; the conclusions belong to the reader.

Where should I start?With the Healthy Republic series — Civic Virtue first — or, for the founding story, The Great Lawgivers. Children start with the Lawgivers School Edition. The four pillars above map the whole territory.

Are there civics games?Yes — the Civic Virtue Climb is live now: infinite, free, GENO-coached, no login, with anonymous score saving. Per the Magic of Six standard, every title on this page earns its own game over time.
